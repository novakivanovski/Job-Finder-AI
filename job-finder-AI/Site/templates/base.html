Welcome to Job Finder AI!

Job Finder AI helps speed up job search by filtering job postings that you are not interested in. This removes the frustration of going over thousands of irrelevant job postings and saves you a lot of time.

How to use

Register an account and login. Click "Train" to begin training the algorithm to look for jobs that align with your skills and interests. Press "Search" to obtain a list of job postings aggregated from multiple sites that are filtered based on your criteria.
